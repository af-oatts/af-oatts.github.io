## Introduction

**This is a temporary privacy policy.** We collect basic analytics data to understand how people use this site. We don't sell this data. You can opt out of cookies at any time.

---

## What we collect

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized usage data — things like which pages get visited, how long people stay, and what country traffic comes from. This helps us understand what's working and what isn't.

We don't collect your name, email, or any information that identifies you as a person.

---

## Cookies

Google Analytics sets cookies to track sessions and recognize returning visitors. When you first visit, we'll ask for your consent before any analytics cookies are set.

You can change or withdraw your cookie consent at any time using the cookie settings link in the footer. We honor all opt-outs under Google Consent Mode v2.

---

## What we never do

- Sell your data to anyone, for any reason.
- Share your data with third parties beyond Google Analytics.
- Use your data for advertising or profiling.

---

## Third-party services

Google Analytics is governed by Google's own privacy policy. You can review it at [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). You can also opt out of Google Analytics across all sites using [Google's opt-out browser add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

---

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. If we do, the updated date at the top will reflect it.
